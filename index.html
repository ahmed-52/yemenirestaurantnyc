Our Story

A Taste of Yemen in the Heart of NYC

Nestled on 125th Street, we bring the warmth and hospitality of Yemeni culture to Harlem. Every dish tells a story — from our signature Fahsa bubbling in traditional clay pots to our giant freshly-baked bread pulled straight from the tandoor oven.

We're more than a restaurant. We're a community gathering place where strangers become friends over shared platters and aromatic spices.
